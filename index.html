Catch!

Capture Colour Effortlessly

Step 1

Open the App and take a photograph of the subject you would like to capture the colour from.

Step 2

Tap on the area you would like to capture colour from, add multiple colours to create a palette, Zoom by pinching two fingers.

Step 3

Click done and there we have it, all your captured colours in one palette for you with their unique hexadecimal codes.
